About NYEKUSA

Our history, mission, and the passionate team that makes it all possible.

Our Mission

NYEKUSA is a Christian fellowship group based in Kenya, committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and building a strong community of believers. We believe in the power of faith, love, and unity to transform lives and communities.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and light in our community, demonstrating God's love through our actions and serving as ambassadors of Christ in all we do.

Our Values

  • Faith: Trusting in God's plan and promises
  • Love: Showing Christ's love to all people
  • Unity: Building strong relationships within our community
  • Service: Serving God and others with humble hearts

Our Activities & Programs

Join us in various spiritual and community-building activities throughout the week.

Sunday Worship Service

Join us every Sunday at 9:00 AM for inspiring worship, powerful sermons, and fellowship. Experience God's presence in a welcoming environment.

Bible Study Groups

Wednesday evening Bible study sessions where we dive deep into God's word and grow together in understanding and faith.

Youth Ministry

Engaging programs for young people including sports, music, drama, and leadership development activities every Friday evening.

Women's Fellowship

Monthly gatherings for women to share, pray, and support each other through various life seasons and challenges.

Community Outreach

Regular community service projects including feeding programs, clothing drives, and visiting orphanages and elderly care centers.

Prayer Meetings

Join us for powerful prayer sessions every Tuesday evening as we intercede for our community, nation, and world.
